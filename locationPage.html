Pop-Up Locations

We are working with local bar managers to initiate pop-ups that will introduce our food to a variety of customers in St. Louis. At this time, the schedule below is where you can find us!



Parlor, 4170 Manchester Ave, St. Louis, MO 63110


Located in The Grove, Parlor is one of the most friendly atmospheres around. Come hangout at one of St. Louis's best barcades and enjoy some Weekend at Baudy's goods.
We will be parked in the parking lot on Friday and Saturday evenings beginning at 6pm until sold out! Come chase the flavor!





Sasha's Wine Bars, 706 De Mun Ave #2238, St. Louis, MO 63105


Another local favorite has granted us permission to park our truck on site! Come support two local restaurants, enjoy tasty food, and the best wines in the city!
We will begin serving at 5pm until sold out on Tuesdays and Sundays!




Friendly's, 3503 Roger Pl, St. Louis, MO 63116

Friendly's Sports Bar & Grill, located in Tower Grove South offers a laid-back South City ambiance that's ideal for unwinding and watching the game. If you'd prefer enjoy a match on your own, there's pool and darts on the premises, or you can step outside to the massive beer garden and play air hockey, washers, pop-a-shot, and a number of other games. Apart from that, Friendly's beer garden has its own bar, an abundance of televisions, and a warmed shelter for those chilly days.
You can find us here on Monday nights starting at 7pm until sold out!
