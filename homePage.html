About The Truck

Weekend at Baudy's is a mobile kitchen, where you can enjoy your delicious Mexican dishes at your favorite locations in St. Louis.
Through this food truck, we try to bring coworkers, friends, family members, and strangers together in surprising and happy ways. Moments like when the aroma of Strip Steak Roll mixes with colleagues' lunchtime babble; when friends play an spontaneous game of rock, paper, scissors while waiting for their orders to be called, and a welcoming face offering some refreshing Jarrito drinks.
We promise over more than simply food; we also share extraordinary experiences.
